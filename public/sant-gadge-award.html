About the Award

A Tribute to Selfless Service

Our Vision

To encourage and celebrate every individual who dedicates their life to serving society — embodying the timeless values of Sant Gadge Maharaj.

Our Mission

Recognize unsung heroes, inspire communities, and promote a deep sense of social responsibility through cleanliness, compassion, and collective welfare.

Our History

Running proudly since 2021, the Sant Gadge Seva Award has honored frontline professionals, community workers, and changemakers — including many who served selflessly during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Award Categories

Honoring Two Pillars of Service

Corona Warriors

Honoring NDRF, RPF, Police, AIIMS doctors, nurses, and medical staff who stood as the nation's shield through the darkest days of the pandemic.

Social Workers

Celebrating community organizers, awareness campaigners, relief volunteers, and citizens devoted to public welfare and grassroots transformation.

Our Journey

Six Years of Service

2021

The Sant Gadge Seva Award is founded — honoring frontline COVID warriors across Bengal.

2022

First state-wide ceremony at Barrackpore; over 50 social workers felicitated.

2023

Launch of plantation drives and Durga Puja community programs across 10 districts.

2024

Women's Day celebrations and student essay & drawing competitions added.

2025

Crossed 200 awardees milestone; expanded outreach to neighboring states.

2026

Sixth edition at Sukanta Sadan — the biggest celebration of seva yet.
