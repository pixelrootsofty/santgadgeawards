Program 06 — Charity

Community Outreach

Distributing essentials, organising health camps, and standing alongside families in need — the year-round backbone of our work.

Service That Doesn't Wait for an Occasion

Between award ceremonies and festivals, our volunteers run quiet, year-round outreach across Bengal — distributing food, clothes, medicines and school supplies wherever the need is greatest.

We work hand-in-hand with local welfare societies, hospitals and panchayats. No publicity, no banners — just the steady delivery of help in the spirit Sant Gadge Maharaj himself embodied.

What Our Outreach Includes

Outreach is needs-led — we listen to local committees first, then deploy volunteers, supplies or specialists as required.

  • Essentials distribution — rice, dal, hygiene kits and blankets during peak seasons
  • Health camps — free check-ups, eye care, and basic medicines in under-served wards
  • Family support — school fees, ration assistance and emergency aid for families in crisis
  • Disaster response — rapid relief teams during floods, cyclones and pandemics
